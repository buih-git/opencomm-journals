Open Media and Communication Research

A curated list of journals for open science in media and communication research

Created by Moritz Büchi [github | profile], Michael Reiss, and Tobias Dienlin.
Last update: 04/2026.

Inclusion criteria: Open science features (hybrid open access alone does not qualify), peer-reviewed, publishes English language articles, focused on Media and Communication generally, on subdisciplines (e.g., science communication, media psychology, etc.), or generalist / other disciplinary journals open to Media and Communication (e.g., PLOS ONE)

Feedback and corrections welcome via GitHub Issues.
